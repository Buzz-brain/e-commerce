FAQ

How do I setup my business on Targetpreneur E-Commerce?

To set up your business on Targetpreneur E-Commerce, simply click on the "Register" button and follow the instructions provided. You will be asked to provide some basic information about your business, such as your name, phone number, URL, email address, and password. Additionally, you will need to select how you heard about us.

How do I post a product on Targetpreneur E-Commerce?

To post a product on our E-Commerce, log in to your dashboard and click on the "Products" tab. From there, click on the "Add New Product" button and fill in the required information, such as the product details, category, purchase price, selling price, and images. You can also specify if the product has any variations, as well as the quantity available.
